Welcome to the official schedule for TechCon 2024! This year’s conference spans three exciting days of keynote speeches, panel discussions, interactive workshops, networking sessions, and product showcases. Whether you're an industry veteran or a first-time attendee, the schedule is designed to offer something for everyone — from emerging trends in AI and cybersecurity to hands-on coding labs and inspirational talks from top tech leaders. Check the timetable below to plan your experience and make the most of every moment.

Detailed Schedule of Events for TechCon 2024
Time Session Speaker Location Description
09:00 - 10:00 Opening Keynote: The Future of Innovation Dr. Aisha Kiplagat Main Hall Kick-off address exploring global tech shifts and innovation trends.
10:15 - 11:00 AI in Healthcare Prof. David Mwangi Room A How machine learning is transforming patient care and diagnostics.
11:15 - 12:00 Women in Tech Leadership Panel: Various Speakers Room B A powerful panel on inclusion, equity, and growth in tech careers.
12:00 - 13:30 Lunch & Expo Tour - Expo Hall Network with sponsors and explore new tools, startups, and projects.
13:30 - 14:30 Cybersecurity in the AI Age Naomi Wanjiku (CTO, SecureNet) Main Hall Addressing evolving threats and proactive protection with AI.
14:45 - 15:30 Hands-on Workshop: Build a Web App with JS Kevin Otieno Dev Lab Practical coding workshop — bring your laptop and get building!
15:45 - 16:30 Fireside Chat: Ethics in Tech Alex Kimani & Dr. Rose Njeri Room C A candid conversation about responsibility and human-centered design.
16:45 - 17:30 Startups Showcase 5 Startup Founders (Pitch Format) Room B Lightning talks from selected startups disrupting their industries.
17:30 - 18:00 Day 1 Wrap-Up & Announcements Host MC Main Hall Summary of the day, key takeaways, and next-day highlights.
